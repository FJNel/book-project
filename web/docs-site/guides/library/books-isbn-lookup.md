# ISBN Lookup And Suggestions

Use this guide when you want help understanding what happens after you enter an ISBN on the Add Book page.

It explains what the lookup can fill in for you, what automatic matches mean, what suggestions mean, and what to expect if the lookup cannot finish.

## What ISBN Lookup Does

ISBN lookup is an optional helper on the Add Book page.

When you enter an ISBN and click **Lookup ISBN**, the app tries to find book information for you and fill in as much of the form as it can safely use.

This can save time, especially when a book already has clear public information attached to its ISBN.

## What ISBN Lookup Does Not Do

ISBN lookup does not save the book for you.

It also does not skip the normal Add Book process.

You still:

- review the details,
- make any corrections you want,
- complete the copy section,
- use the normal Review and Confirm steps.

Nothing is saved until you finish the usual Add Book flow.

## Where You Use It

ISBN lookup appears only when you are adding a new book.

It does not appear when you are editing an existing book.

That means:

- Add Book can use ISBN lookup,
- Edit Book stays focused on updating information that is already saved.

## How It Works From Your Point Of View

1. Enter the book’s ISBN in Step 1.
2. Click **Lookup ISBN**.
3. A loading window appears while the app searches for book information.
4. If the lookup succeeds, the page fills in details and may link or suggest related items.
5. You review the results and continue with the normal Add Book steps.

## What The Lookup May Fill In

Depending on what information is available, the app may fill in:

- Title
- Subtitle
- ISBN
- Publication Date
- Number of Pages
- Book Cover Image URL
- Book Description

You can still change any of these before saving.

## Automatic Matches And Suggestions

After a lookup, you may notice two different kinds of help on the page.

### Automatic matches

Sometimes the app clearly recognises something you already have in your library.

For example, it may recognise:

- an author you already added,
- a publisher you already use,
- a book type you already created,
- a series already in your library,
- a language already available,
- a tag you already use.

When the match is clear, the app may add it for you automatically.

You will usually see a short message in the lookup section telling you what was linked.

### Suggestions

If the app is not sure, or if it finds something new that is not already in your library, it shows a suggestion instead of making the choice for you.

Suggestions appear in the part of the page where they belong, such as:

- Book Type
- Authors
- Publisher
- Series
- Tags

That way, you can review each suggestion in the right section of the form.

## Book Type Suggestions

If the app thinks the book belongs to a type you do not already have, you may see a Book Type suggestion.

You may see:

- the suggested type name,
- a short description when available.

You can:

- approve it and have it added and selected for this book,
- dismiss it and choose your own book type instead.

If the app clearly recognises a book type you already use, it may select that automatically instead of showing a new suggestion.

## Publisher Suggestions

If the app finds a publisher that is not already in your library, you may see a suggestion in the Publisher section.

You may see:

- the publisher name,
- a founded date if one is available,
- a website if one is available,
- notes if any helpful extra detail is available.

You can approve the suggestion to add and select that publisher, or dismiss it and choose a publisher yourself.

## Author Suggestions

If the app finds author information that is not already clearly matched in your library, it can show one or more author suggestions.

You may see:

- display name,
- first names,
- surname,
- date of birth,
- date of death or whether the author is still living,
- a short biography,
- a suggested role for this book.

Before approving a suggested author, you can review the role and change it if needed.

For example, you might choose:

- Author
- Editor
- Illustrator
- Translator
- Other

If you approve a suggested author, the app adds that author and links them to the book in the same way as if you had added them manually.

If you dismiss a suggestion, only that suggestion is removed.

## Series Suggestions

Some books may show a suggested series.

You may see:

- series name,
- a short description,
- a website if available,
- the book’s order within the series if known.

If you approve the suggestion, the series is added and linked to the book.

If you dismiss it, only that suggestion is removed.

Not every book will produce a series suggestion.

## Tag Suggestions

The app may also suggest tags that seem useful for the book.

These appear as small suggestion labels in the Tags section.

You can:

- add a suggested tag,
- dismiss a suggested tag,
- ignore suggestions and add your own tags manually.

## Why Some Things Are Linked Automatically While Others Are Only Suggested

The app tries to be careful.

If a match is clear, it may link it automatically to save you time.

If the app is not sure, it prefers to ask you instead of making the wrong choice.

This is why you may see:

- one author linked automatically,
- another author shown as a suggestion,
- a publisher selected automatically,
- but no series suggestion at all.

That is normal.

## Loading, Progress, And Timeouts

When you start a lookup, a loading window appears.

The progress bar moves while the lookup is in progress.

If the lookup finishes normally, the loading window closes and the results are applied to the page.

If the lookup takes too long, it times out after about 10 seconds.

When that happens:

- the loading window closes,
- the page becomes usable again,
- you see a message explaining that the lookup took too long,
- you can try again or continue manually.

## If The Lookup Finds Nothing

Sometimes an ISBN does not have enough information available.

If that happens, you may see a message explaining that no results were found.

You can still continue adding the book manually.

## If Information Looks Incomplete Or Wrong

Public book information is not always complete.

You may find that:

- some books only have a year instead of a full publication date,
- the description is short or missing,
- no useful cover image is available,
- series information is missing,
- some details need correction.

That is why the Add Book page always lets you review and edit the results before saving.

## Common Questions

### Is ISBN lookup required?

No. It is optional.

You can ignore it and fill in the Add Book page manually.

### Can I still edit the fields after a lookup?

Yes.

The lookup helps fill in the form, but you remain in control of the final details.

### Does the app save suggestions automatically?

No.

If the app clearly recognises something you already have, it may link that existing item automatically.

But new suggestions still need your approval.

### What if I do not want to use any of the suggestions?

That is fine.

You can dismiss them and continue with your own choices.

### Why did one suggestion appear in a section lower down the page?

Suggestions appear in the section they belong to.

For example, author suggestions appear in Authors, and publisher suggestions appear in Publisher.

### Why do some books have fewer lookup results than others?

Different books have different amounts of public information available.

Some books have rich details attached to their ISBN, while others have only the basics.

### What happens if the lookup fails?

You can still add the book manually.

The lookup is a helper, not a requirement.

### Can I use ISBN lookup while editing a book I already saved?

No.

ISBN lookup is part of Add Book only.
