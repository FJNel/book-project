# Change your Theme

The Book Project allows you to customize the appearance of the web interface by choosing from Light, Dark, or System Default themes. 

## Why Change the Theme?

Changing the theme can enhance your reading experience, reduce eye strain, and align the interface with your personal preferences or the ambient lighting conditions. The Dark theme, for example, is often preferred in low-light environments, while the Light theme may be more suitable for well-lit settings. 

## How to Change the Theme

To change the theme of The Book Project, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Book Project account on the [login page](https://bookproject.fjnel.co.za/).
2. Once you're on your [dashboard](https://bookproject.fjnel.co.za/dashboard), click on the "Account" button in the navigation menu.
3. On the Account page, navigate to the "Profile" tab.
4. Scroll down to the "Theme" section. Here you will see a dropdown menu with the available theme options: Light, Dark, and Use device setting. 
5. Select your preferred theme from the dropdown menu. 
6. Once you've selected your desired theme, click the "Save theme" button at the bottom of the page.
7. After a few moments, your theme will be updated, and the interface will reflect your choice. You will also see a confirmation message indicating that your theme has been saved successfully. 

## Questions

### What themes are available in The Book Project?
The Book Project offers three themes: Light, Dark, and System Default (which matches your device's theme settings).

### What does "Use device setting" mean?
Choosing "Use device setting" allows The Book Project to automatically adjust its theme based on your device's current theme preference. If your device is set to Dark mode, The Book Project will also display in Dark mode, and vice versa for Light mode.

### How does The Book Project detect my device's theme?
The Book Project uses standard web technologies to detect your device's theme preference. It checks the operating system or browser settings to determine whether Dark mode or Light mode is enabled and adjusts the interface accordingly when "Use device setting" is selected.

### Can I change the theme back to my previous choice?
Yes, you can change the theme at any time by following the same steps outlined above. Simply select your desired theme from the dropdown menu and save your changes.

### Are theme changes applied immediately?
Yes, once you select a new theme and save your changes, the interface will update to reflect your choice almost immediately.

### Do I need to refresh the page for the theme change to take effect?
No, the theme change should take effect automatically without needing to refresh the page. However, if you encounter any issues, refreshing the page can help ensure the new theme is applied correctly.

### Will my theme preference be remembered next time I log in?
Yes, The Book Project saves your theme preference to your account settings, so your chosen theme will be applied automatically the next time you log in.

### Does the theme apply to all pages within The Book Project?
Yes, the selected theme will be applied consistently across all pages and sections of The Book Project, providing a uniform experience throughout the application. Your theme will also be applied to pages that do not required you to be logged in, such as the login and registration pages; however, you will not be able to change the theme on those pages.

### Does changing the theme affect the functionality of The Book Project?
No, changing the theme only affects the visual appearance of the interface. All features and functionalities of The Book Project remain unchanged regardless of the selected theme.

### Can I customize the themes further?
Currently, The Book Project does not offer additional customization options beyond the three available themes. However, future updates may include more customization features based on user feedback.

### Is the theme change specific to my account or device?
The theme change is specific to your account. This means that your selected theme will be applied whenever you log in to The Book Project from any device or browser. 

### I use different themes on different devices. Will The Book Project remember my choice for each device?
No, The Book Project saves your theme preference to your account settings, so the same theme will be applied across all devices when you log in. If you want each device's theme to match its settings, choose "Use device setting" as your theme option.

### Why does the app look different on my device compared to others?
The appearance of The Book Project may vary based on the selected theme and the device's display settings. If you have chosen "Use device setting," the app will adapt to the theme preferences of each device, which can lead to differences in appearance across devices.

### Can I switch themes while using The Book Project?
Yes, you can switch themes at any time by following the steps outlined above. The change will take effect immediately after saving your new preference.
